About Us




ALOHA BAR is not only the oldest and one of the most famous drink bar on Soledad Archipelago, it's the only one. Sorrounded by hundreds of miles of nothing but the ocean, it is the best (and only!) place to drink exquisite cocktails in-between paddling your makeshift raft to safety. Find us and enjoy a moment of peace.
